ABOUT US

HANDS UP CREW is a combination of basketball and football freestyle. This interesting and unique
combination brings an unforgettable show that is full of great tricks, moves and entertainment. Show
can be followed by workshop and many different competitions. There is also a possibility to do school
of basketball and football freestyle after every show. Programs of our crew are suitable for all age
groups and various occasions. Lenght of the show and exhibition is adaptable. It is also possible to
divide the show into shorter exhibitions. You can choose from several choreographies accopmpanied
by music. Experience an unforgettable program of HANDS UP CREW, the communication with an
audience and lots of fun!
