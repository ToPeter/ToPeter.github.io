SPORT WITHOUT LIMITS

SPORT WITHOUT LIMITS presents a special program for schools. Main idea is to present the importance
and benefits of sport for the development of personality. We go to schools and present this interesting way of
physical activity. Purpose is to raise the profile of this new interesting sport and also show young people a new
way of self-realization, motivate them to physical activity in the form of experience. Usual length of our
program is 60 minutes (you can also customize the length of the program as required, for example 45 minutes
or even longer model with multiple competitions and sports games). The most appropriate place to implement
the program is gym (gymnasium or hall), but it is not necessary. The program can be also done on any larger
place or room. The program includes:
· HANDS UP Crew exhibitions,
· lecture on the importance of sport and movement (and how it helped us to fulfill our dreams)
· competitions and sports games,
· entertainment disciplines,
· workshop (the opportunity to learn some basics and try freestyle).

SPORT EXHIBITION

Sports exhibitions (freestyle show) is a dynamic show full of freestyle tricks accompanied by music. The
audience is always part of the exhibitions and also use of props that gives a fun character to the whole show.
There is also a possibility to do longer program or show or divide it into a number of shorter exhibitions. This
type of program is recommended for: sport events (matches, tournaments, championships), half-time shows,
children's events and birthday parties, corporate events, road shows, promotions, parties, festivals or any event
with accompanying program.
EXHIBITION IN ELEGANT CLOTHES

Exhibition is elegant clothes are exhibitions and shows in elegant clothes that are adapted to music and combine elegance
and freestyle. Program includes the use of props and cooperation with the audience.  Length of the program can be
customized according to any requirements. This type of program is recommended for: balls, themed events, weddings,
conferences, congresses, exhibitions and any others.
